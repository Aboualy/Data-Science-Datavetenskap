
The aim of labs is to create a data pipeline or to create an end-to-end analytic setup. 

With the explosion of "Big Data" problems, analytics has become a particularly intriguing field in many scientific areas as well as in marketing, finance and other business disciplines. People with analytical and in particular statistical learning skills are in high demand. In this course, we will introduce the essential techniques for summarizing, modelling and understanding complex datasets, and we strive to keep the course materials theoretically sound and practically relevant. In the first half of the course, the classic approaches to statistical learning and data mining such as linear regression, logistic regression and classification and regression trees will be exhibited. It is important to point out that we concentrate more on the applications of these methods and less on the mathematical details.
